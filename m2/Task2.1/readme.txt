1. The most popular hypervisors are Hyper-V, KVW, VMware and virtual box. 

2. VirtualBox, KVW, and VMware Workstation are Layer 2 hypervisors. Hyper-V and VMware ESXi are Layer 1 hypervisors.

The main difference is that a Level 2 hypervisor is an application that runs on an existing OS, while a Level 1 hypervisor is the OS itself.
